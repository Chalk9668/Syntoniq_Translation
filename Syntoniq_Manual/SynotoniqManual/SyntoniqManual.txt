SYNTONIQ MANUAL

This is the manual for Syntoniq. Syntoniq converts musical notation in text files to Csound or MIDI output. It was designed from the beginning to represent music in any tuning system, which makes it ideal for use with microtonal music. Syntoniq can generate MIDI using MPE (MIDI Polyphonic Expression) with pitch-bend, specifically designed to be friendly to import into a Digital Audio Workstation for further refinement.
How to Read This Manual

The manual is divided into multiple parts. Parts are denoted by part chapters whose titles are ALL UPPERCASE. Later sections of the manual build upon earlier sections. Not all parts of the manual will matter to all people. When browsing the docs, be sure to read the part chapter for that part.

The manual contains some text marked with FUTURE. This serves the dual purpose of being a reminder in the manual of things to update as we add new features and a signal to the reader that a potential change may be coming.

# SYNTONIQ 取扱説明書

 Syntoniqにようこそ。SyontoniqはCLI上で動作するCsound&MIDIエディタです。貴方の思うあらゆる調律が可能で、殊に微分音音楽に適するよう設計されています。MIDIコントローラを用いての編集も可能で、MPEに対応したMIDIの書き出し、Csoundでの書き出しなど、表現の幅に富みます。そして貴方のお好みのDAWに、こちらで生成した音楽をインポートすることにより、更なる調整もお手のものになっております！！
